JuliaHEP 2024

JuliaHEP is back! After the successful 2023 event at ECAP last year, JuliaHEP comes to CERN from 30 September to 4 October for the 2024 edition.

Registration and abstract submission are now open. You can help advertise the event with this poster.
Before the JuliaHEP workshop there will be a three day tutorial on Julia in HEP - you will be able to sign up soon for that training.

Activities

We organise many activities, from our working groups, to organising events, to supporting projects as HSF projects, and helping communication within the community through our discussion forums and technical notes.

The HSF can also write letters of collaboration and cooperation to project proposals.

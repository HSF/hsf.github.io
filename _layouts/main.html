ML4SCI in GSoC 2022

The ML4Sci open source organization is participating in the 2022 Google Summer of Code. If you are a student interested in our projects please check our ideas page. ML4Sci is an umbrella organization that welcomes other projects and organizations related to machine-learning for science. Please contact the admins at ml4-sci@cern.ch if you are interested in participating as a project.

Please take a look at our GSoC Page for more details.

If you are interested in our activities please join our announcements mailing list. To join, you will need to create a CERN lightweight account.

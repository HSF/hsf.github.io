We are delighted to announce that the next joint WLCG/HSF Workshop will take place in Bologna, Italy, from 2-6 November 2026.

[https://indico.cern.ch/e/wlcg-hsf-2026](https://indico.cern.ch/e/wlcg-hsf-2026)

The workshop will address key developments and strategic directions in distributed computing for high-energy physics, bringing together experts from WLCG, HSF, and the broader community through a mix of plenary and parallel sessions.

The HSF will have sessions on Community Software, sustainable software, Training, and other topics of broad interest at the workshop.
We invite you to submit abstracts for talks of 20' where you can describe a tool, package or other contribution that you are making to software for multiple experiments.

[https://indico.cern.ch/event/1655190/abstracts/](https://indico.cern.ch/event/1655190/abstracts/)


#### **Important dates**

* Abstract submission deadline: 15 September 2026
* Workshop registration deadline: 10 October 2026

#### **Contact**

For any queries, please contact `wlcg-hsf-workshop-2026@lists.cnaf.infn.it`.

We very much look forward to welcoming you to Bologna in November for an engaging week of discussion, collaboration, and exchange across our communities.

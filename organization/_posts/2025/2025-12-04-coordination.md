### Steering Group

Meeting held on December 2nd. Several matters discussed, see the minutes online on the HSF website (in the next few days).
Some quick points from what got discussed:
- Meeting held recently with the IML conveners.
    - Thank you the several of you AA conveners that connected. We were 10 connected with IML colleagues, which is pretty nice.
    - We touched on the scope of the HSF and IML.
    - There was consensus that we could and should engage more firmly in the future, and both parties will have that in mind and will be in touch when relevant, e.g. for future IML workshops that could have a training component, for joint seminars, etc.
- Extension of the AG members. News in due time.
- Status of Affiliation programme, see details below.
